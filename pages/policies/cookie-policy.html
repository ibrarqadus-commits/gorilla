2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device that help us improve your browsing experience. They allow us to remember your preferences, understand how visitors use our site, and improve website functionality.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for the operation of our website (e.g., session management, navigation).
  • Performance Cookies: Collect information about how visitors use our website to help us improve content and usability.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember choices you make to personalise your experience.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block cookies entirely or to delete those already stored. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

Currently, The Streets of London does not use analytics or advertising cookies from third parties. If this changes, our Cookie Policy will be updated accordingly to reflect any new tracking technology or providers.
